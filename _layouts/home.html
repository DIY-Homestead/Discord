Rules

  • Be respectful to fellow posters – name-calling, rudeness, and trolling are not welcome here.
  • Do not ask for or give any personal information on anyone in a public channel, same rules as reddit apply.
  • Keep chat clean, no NSFW/porn.
  • Do not spam for ranks. Spammers will be deranked and banned.
  • Only use bot commands in #bots. Repeated failure to do so may result in a kick.
  • Emojis in usernames will be removed.
  • You may be banned at any time for hookup requests or blatant trolling.
  • Be civil and friendly above all else.

Role Explanation

  • Each time you post a message you get a random number of XP between 15 and 25.
  • To avoid flood, you can only gain xp once per minute. In the server, you can type !rank or !levels to get your rank or see leaderboards.
  • Level 0: Everyone starts at level zero, you should be able to comment in #general.
  • The admins are working to bring a fun reward system for gaining levels. Higher levels will have access to secret channels and other goodies. Stay tuned
  • We may expand other levels and roles as needed.
  • Vanity roles like ‘Homesteader’, ‘Urban Homesteader’ and ‘Enthusiast’, can be self-assigned by typing exactly “/homesteader” or “/enthusiast” in #bots without the quotes. If you have any problems assigning yourself a role, feel free to ping the moderators.
